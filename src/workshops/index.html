Strategic Design

Eric Evans

Some design decisions have an impact on the trajectory of the whole project. This course delves into principles for clarifying the big picture, getting effort focused on the core, and coordinating multi-team development.

Essential Domain-Driven Design

Paul Rayner

It's easy to get lost in the patterns and acronyms and miss the essence of domain-driven design (DDD). In this workshop you'll gain an understanding of what DDD looks like, why it matters, and how to apply it to you, your team, and your organization.
