Essential Domain-Driven Design

Paul Rayner

Domain-driven design has a reputation for being complex, with layers of patterns, acronyms, and conflicting advice. This workshop cuts through the noise and gives you a solid foundation in the ideas, techniques, and skills that make DDD work in real project contexts.
