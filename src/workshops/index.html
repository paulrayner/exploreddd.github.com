Pre-Conference Workshops

Monday-Tuesday, September 21-22, 2026

Workshop tickets sold separately from the conference.

Essential Domain-Driven Design

Paul Rayner

Domain-driven design has a reputation for being complex, with layers of patterns, acronyms, and conflicting advice. This workshop cuts through the noise and gives you a solid foundation in the ideas, techniques, and skills that make DDD work in real project contexts.

Designing Microservices: Responsibilities, APIs and Collaborations

Chris Richardson

The microservice architecture enables fast flow at scale, but it is far too easy to get service boundaries wrong and create a fragile distributed monolith. In this workshop, you will learn a structured, collaborative process for defining a microservice architecture with clear responsibilities, well-defined APIs, and explicit collaboration patterns.

AI-Assisted Software Architecture & Domain Discovery

Indu Alagarsamy

AI won't simplify a complex system. But it can dramatically accelerate your understanding. Over two full days, learn how to use AI tools as a force multiplier for architectural and domain discovery in complex existing systems.

Build Your Own Coding Agent from Scratch

Matt Wynne & Steven Diamante

You use AI agents every day, but do you know what's actually happening under the hood? Build an agent from scratch, without any frameworks. Watch a basic chatbot evolve into a sophisticated agent with tools, conversation history, and guardrails, then explore evals, deterministic vs non-deterministic capabilities, and a competition between the agents you've built.
