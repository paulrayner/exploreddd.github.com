About the Hotel

Catbird is not your typical hotel. It's an independent, design-forward hotel with condo-style suites, full kitchens, and a creative community vibe that matches the spirit of our conference. Each suite features a lofted bed, 4K projector (instead of a TV in most rooms), and a layout that feels more like home. For attendees staying the full week for both workshops and the conference, it's a comfortable base with everything you need.

On-site amenities include rooftop hot tubs, the Rook Rooftop bar, Catbird Kitchen, a Peloton fitness center, laundry on every floor, and outdoor gear rentals. The hotel is also pet-friendly. Browse the photo gallery →

The Neighborhood

RiNo is Denver's creative district, known for its independent restaurants, breweries, coffee shops, street art, and live music. Everything is walkable from the hotel.

Rates and Booking

Studios start from approximately ~$260/night all-in for conference week (Sep 21-25), including the $198 room rate, $30 daily amenity fee, and Denver taxes. The amenity fee covers breakfast for two, wifi, a $15 Rook Rooftop credit, laundry, and fitness center access.

Staying for workshops too? Stays of 6+ nights get a reduced amenity fee ($25/night instead of $30), bringing the all-in cost closer to ~$255/night.

Denver is a popular destination in September. Book early to secure your spot.

Getting to the Venue

Catbird Hotel is approximately 2 miles from the conference venue at CSU Spur, in the same north Denver corridor.

By Car (~7 min) →

A quick rideshare along the north Denver corridor.

By Bike (~10 min) →

An easy ride through north Denver. Lime e-bikes are available throughout the area.

From the Airport

Transport from Denver International Airport (DEN) to the hotel is easy by car or train.

By Car (~35 min) →

Uber/Lyft typically $35-45. Compare both apps for best price.

By Train (~40 min) →

RTD A Line: $10.50 day pass. DEN to 38th/Blake, then a short walk.
