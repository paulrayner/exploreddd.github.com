Hotel

We've partnered with the Catbird Hotel as the recommended hotel for Explore DDD 2026. Located in Denver's RiNo (River North Art District), Catbird is an independent, design-forward hotel with condo-style suites, full kitchens, and a creative community vibe that matches the spirit of our conference.

Catbird Hotel
3770 Walnut Street
Denver, Colorado 80205

About the Hotel

Catbird is not your typical hotel. Each suite features a full kitchen, lofted bed, 4K projector (instead of a TV in most rooms), and a condo-style layout that feels more like home. For attendees staying the full week for both workshops and the conference, it's a comfortable base with everything you need.

On-site amenities include rooftop hot tubs, the Rook Rooftop bar, Catbird Kitchen, a Peloton fitness center, laundry on every floor, and bicycle and Vespa rentals. The hotel is also pet-friendly.

The Neighborhood

RiNo is Denver's creative district, known for its independent restaurants, breweries, coffee shops, street art, and live music. Walkable evening options make it easy to continue conversations from the conference over dinner and drinks.

Catbird is approximately 2 miles from the conference venue at CSU Spur, in the same north Denver corridor. Getting between the hotel and venue is easy via rideshare, bike, or the river trail.

Book Early

Denver is a popular destination in September. Book early to secure your spot.

Transport from Denver International Airport to downtown is easily available via train, Uber, Lyft, or taxi.
