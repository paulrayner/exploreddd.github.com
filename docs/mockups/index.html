Key Differentiators

  • Quality over quantity - every session designed for interaction
  • Speaker participation - experts available throughout, not just during talks
  • Hands-on workshops with immediate skill application
  • Small group sizes for personalized attention
  • Structured learning - no overwhelming options
  • Direct expert access for technical questions

Conference Focus Areas

  • Sociotechnical Architecture - technology + human factors
  • AI-Enhanced Development - practical generative AI integration
  • Strategic Domain Modeling - DDD in complex systems
  • Legacy Modernization - evolving architectures safely
  • Hands-on Problem Solving - real challenges with expert guidance

Conference Format

Pre-Conference Workshops (2 Days)

  • Separate registration required
  • Deep-dive technical training
  • Small groups with expert facilitators
  • Real architectural challenges
  • Reached capacity in 2025

Conference Sessions (2 Days)

  • Included with conference ticket
  • Expert-led with hands-on components
  • Multiple training tracks
  • All sessions reached capacity in 2025
  • Immediate skill application focus
