Only Explore DDD brings together the world's best software architects, developers, technical leaders, and industry experts for three days of in-depth workshops, best-in-class learning and unparalleled community. Will you be there?

"Attending Explore DDD really accelerated our understanding of how to better model our business domains and better architect not just our product but also our teams."

— Alex Gemmell, PatientPop

7 REASONS TO ATTEND

  • Interact and engage with world-class speakers
  • Be part of an international community of practicing software architects, industry experts, developers, and technical leaders
  • Explore new ideas and challenge your thinking in an open, sharing, and collaborative environment
  • Expand your skills from hands-on sessions in software modeling and design, taught by industry-leading pioneers, practitioners and innovators
  • Customize your professional development with specialized, pre-conference workshops
  • Learn in an inspiring setting, surrounded by views of the majestic Rocky mountains
  • Observe domain modeling in action and learn alongside your peers at the only conference of its kind in North America

Explore DDD is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, or religion (or lack thereof). Read the full Code of Conduct on the about page.

To increase diversity at Explore DDD we are offering scholarships to people from underrepresented groups in tech who wouldn’t otherwise be able to attend. If you are a member of a group underrepresented within the tech community we encourage you to apply.

Would assistance with childcare expenses make it easier for you to attend the conference? We’d like to help. Please let us know at contact@exploreddd.com
